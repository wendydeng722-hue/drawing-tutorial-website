How to Draw an Anime Head (Step by Step)

Step 1: Draw a Circle

Start by drawing a simple circle.

This circle will be the top part of the head.

Step 2: Add the Chin

Draw two short lines going down from the sides of the circle.

Then connect them with a soft “V” shape to form the chin.

Now you have the basic head shape.

Step 3: Draw Guidelines

Draw one vertical line in the middle of the face.

Then draw one horizontal line across the center.

These lines help you place the eyes and keep the face balanced.

Step 4: Draw the Eyes

Draw the eyes on the horizontal line.

Anime eyes are usually big and round.

Leave some space between the two eyes.

Step 5: Add the Nose and Mouth

Draw a small line for the nose below the eyes.

Then draw a short curved line for the mouth.

Keep them simple.

Step 6: Draw the Ears

Draw the ears on the sides of the head.

They usually start from the eye line and end near the nose line.

Step 7: Add Hair

Start with big shapes first, then add smaller lines for details.

Step 8: Clean the Drawing

Erase the guidelines and darken the final lines.
